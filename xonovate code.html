Xonovate Code Champions Program

We believe in shaping the future by empowering the next generation of tech leaders. Our Code Champions Program is dedicated to nurturing young minds and cultivating the innovators of tomorrow. Through practical coding exercises, mentorship, and real-world projects like this, we provide students with the skills and confidence they need to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology. Mark worked diligently to design, code, and deliver this fully functional website, showcasing his growing expertise and passion.
